Privacy Policy

Jumpforce does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The extension operates entirely within your browser and interacts only with the Salesforce pages you are already using. All actions (like opening pages or detecting record IDs) are executed locally and are never sent to any external server.

We respect your privacy and are committed to ensuring that your data stays within your control. If you have any questions, feel free to reach out via the contact option provided in the extension listing.
